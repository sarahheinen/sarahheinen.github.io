WiCS Hackathon 2022

February 26-27, 2022
Demonstration of Gameplay

I participated in a hackathon put on by Women in Computer Science at UT Austin. I participated with their virtual option, joining workshops via Zoom. Participants had a weekend to put together and submit their projects.

The category I submitted my project for was "Carnival Hacks". I wrote a Python text-based game based on the matching ducks carnival game. In this game, the player selects two ducks from the pool, and if the ducks have matching numbers, the player wins a prize.
