Audition for Sacred Music Program

May 8, 2020
Sonata in C minor - Rondo

I played this piece for a remote audition for the Sacred Music Program at Franciscan University. This is the third movement of Beethoven's Pathetique, or the Rondo movement of his Sonata in C Minor. In addition to this piece, I played J.S. Bach's Invention no. 14.


First Place in Denison Performing Arts Piano Competition

April 27, 2019
Sarah Heinen

The 2019 Denison Performing Arts Piano Competition was hosted at Grayson College. I competed in 10th grade and won first place, under the direction of Claire Dering. I played Scherzo in B flat by Franz Schubert. This was my first (and only) piano competition.
