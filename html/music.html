Audition for Sacred Music Program

May 8, 2020
Sonata in C minor - Rondo

I played this piece for a remote audition for the Sacred Music Program at Franciscan University. This is the third movement of Beethoven's Pathetique, or the Rondo movement of his Sonata in C Minor. In addition to this piece, I played J.S. Bach's Invention no. 14.
